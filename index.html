Baseball cards have been in existence almost as long as the game itself. Originally included as a bonus in cigarette packs, they eventually became popular enough to be sold on their own - with a rectangle of powdery pink gum, of course. Baseball is a beautiful game played by fascinating characters, but best understood through statistics. Baseball cards combine the visual and statistical aspects of the game so perfectly that they've been around for more than 150 years.
